Customer Risk Rating Engine

Score individual and entity KYC risk across six FATF dimensions: customer type, product/service type, delivery channel, geographic risk, transaction behaviour, adverse media exposure. Configurable weighting matrix. Outputs composite score (Low/Medium/High/Prohibited), factor-level breakdown, and risk narrative for onboarding files. Maps to FinCEN Risk-Based Approach, EBA Joint Guidelines, FCA FG17/6, MAS Notice SFA04-N02. Export Markdown risk memo and Policy Mandate JSON. Client-side. Zero PII.
