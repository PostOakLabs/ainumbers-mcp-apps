Agentic Payments Protocol Comparator & Field Crosswalk

The agentic-commerce field is fragmenting into card-network, platform, and crypto camps that all overlap. This tool puts AP2, the Agentic Commerce Protocol (Shared Payment Token), x402, Visa Trusted Agent Protocol, and Mastercard Agent Pay side by side across credential model, what is signed, scope binding, settlement rail, identity substrate, and audit trail — plus a field crosswalk and a scenario recommender.

⚠ These specifications version monthly and figures vary across sources. Reference data here is a June 2026 snapshot for orientation, not authoritative spec text. Re-verify against each protocol's primary source (linked in the output) before designing against it.
